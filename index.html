Nutrition

Nutrition Section

Vegetarian, protein-forward, and built around foods that are easy to find and easy to repeat.

Foods to Eat Daily

  • Milk or dairy/plant-milk
  • Fruit (e.g. banana, apple)
  • Nuts & seeds
  • Legumes (lentils, beans, chickpeas)
  • Protein source (eggs, tofu, paneer, meat, fish)
  • Whole grains (rice, bread, oats)
  • Vegetables
  • Yogurt/curd

Foods to Limit

  • Soft drinks
  • Chips
  • Junk food
  • Too many sweets
